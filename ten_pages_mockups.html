10 Pages a Day — Design Concepts

3 options · Interactive mockups · Tap week cells and month rings

⚡
Habit Pulse — EKG / Vitality

Your 26-week reading history rendered as a heartbeat. Hit weeks spike green above the 70 pg threshold; misses flatline. Habit Score leads with a delta arrow. The week grid and qualification tracker complete the picture. Dark forest-green palette — feels like a health/fitness app but for reading.

✦
Constellation — Star Map / Orbital

52 weeks arranged in 4 concentric rings — the further out, the later in the year. Hit weeks glow bright; early weeks (ring 1) glow gold. Missed weeks are dim. The centre shows Habit Score. Monthly habit rings scroll below. Deep space indigo palette — ceremonial, rare, earned.

⚙
Discipline Engine — Industrial Precision

A mechanical gauge dial for the Habit Score, flanked by 4 instrument readouts. Below, 52 "fuel cells" show the year's weekly output log — charged (hit), partial, or depleted (miss). A monospace score computation log shows exactly how the number is built. Pure black — the most distinctive look in the app.
