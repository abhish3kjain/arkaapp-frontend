Pages Goal Challenge — Design Options

3 interactive mockups · 10,000 pages · 5,600 read · 56% complete

🌊
Option 1 — The Ink Tide

A rising tide of ink that visually fills the screen as pages accumulate. Animated wave shows real-time momentum. Pace comparison card (current vs needed) makes it instantly clear if the reader is ahead or behind.

⚡
Option 2 — Reading Velocity

A bold speedometer shows today's reading pace (pg/day). Momentum dots track consistency streak. A velocity timeline shows monthly pace bars against the "needed" threshold line — instantly surfacing strong vs weak months.

📜
Option 3 — The Chapter Scroll

Each month is an illuminated "chapter" — done months show filled ink columns, current month pulses, future months are ghost outlines. A Chapter Log below shows a narrative read of progress month by month. Feels literary and personal.
